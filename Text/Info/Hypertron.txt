HYPERTRON INSTRUCTIONS

by B. Jones 1987.

  Three important data retrieval robots belonging
to an ancient interplanetary droid colony must be 
resued from the old mine workings on planet
Zennon.  You must pilot your Hypertron and
descend into the perilous planet surface, risking
your own life to save them.

Use the following keys to control the Hypertron:-

q = Left
w = Right
Space = Activate Boosters.

Features include:-

Alien Lifeforms
Underground Caverns
Asteroid Belts
Explosion Detectors
Several multi-screen caverns to explore


                 Good Luck !
